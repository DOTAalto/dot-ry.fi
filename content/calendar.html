Google Calendar

If you have a Google account, visit the following page and click ”Add”: DOT’s Google Calendar

iCal

You can also add the calendar as iCal subscription if you don’t use Google Calendar.

On iOS: go to Settings > Calendar > Accounts > Add Account > Other > Add Subscribed Calendar > Copy and paste the following link: https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/pukg551hc207uqjnjdcgeeuq5o%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics

On other platforms: search how to add an iCal subscription to your calendar app and use the .ics link above.
